Deva Johnson (es_ES)

Deva is originally from the north of Spain. She has combined studies in Translation and Languages Interpreting at the Universidad Pontificia de Comillas (Madrid, Spain) and in Applied Linguistics at the Universidad de Salamanca (Spain) with a Full Stack Software Development Diploma in Code Institute (Ireland). Her extensive experience in localisation includes working as QA Lead for Media Minds, a Wayra 2013 winner company specialised in developping educational apps and housed inside Telefonica's incubator in Madrid, and as QA Software Engineer for Apple in Siri Department. Her attention to detail and solid understanding of software development makes her the perfect match for localising and quality assuring your projects for the Spanish (Spain) market.
